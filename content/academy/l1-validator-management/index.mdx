Welcome to the **L1 Validator Management** course! This course is designed to give you a deep understanding of validator management on Avalanche L1s. By the end of this course, you will have practical skills in deploying an L1 and managing the validator set in a Proof of Authority (PoA) network.

## Course Content

- L1 Setup
- Convert Subnet To Sovereign L1
- `ValidatorManager` deployment (ACP99)
- Configure a new Validator
- Add a Validator
- Remove a Validator
- Change Weight of a Validator
